About Us

Wellbeing Initiative Singapore is an organisation dedicated to improving overall well-being through research, community programs, and outreach. Our work focuses on mental and physical health, with a particular emphasis on women’s health.

Through research, we aim to better understand real needs and challenges in these areas. We then turn those insights into practical, evidence-based community programs and share them through accessible outreach. Our goal is to bridge the gap between research and everyday life so more people can access the support and knowledge they need to live healthier, more informed lives.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to improve well-being through research on mental and physical health, with a focus on women’s health, and to turn those insights into practical community programs and accessible outreach.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has the knowledge and support they need to take care of their mental and physical health, and where research meaningfully improves everyday life.

What We Do

We’re an organisation focused on doing meaningful work through research, community programs, and outreach. We look at issues of well-being, work directly with communities, and share what we learn in simple, practical ways that people can actually use.

Research

Our research focuses on a range of topics related to general well-being, including mental health and physical health, with a particular focus on women’s health.

Community Programs

Based on our research, we develop evidence-based programs that support positive mental and physical health practices within the community.

Outreach

Through talks, workshops, and content creation, we aim to spread awareness, spark conversations, and help more people build better physical and mental well-being.
